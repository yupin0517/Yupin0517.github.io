How to make pancake

Things we need to make pancakes

To make pancake you need to prepare the supplies. We need pancake mix, milk , egg and cooking oil

how to make pancake dough

Add half an egg and (100ml) milk to a large bowl and stir. Add pancake mix and Please keep mixing it so it doesn't form lumps.

Final step of making pancake

Put it on a frying pan over low heat, lightly oil it and wipe it.

Flip it over when bubbles appear while baking.
