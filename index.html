How to make pancake

Things we need to make pancake

  • pancake mix
  • cooking oil
  • egg
  • Milk
  • a bowl

how to make pancake dough

Add half an egg and milk(100ml) to a large bowl and stir.

Add pancake mix and Please keep mixing it so it doesn't form lumps.

Final step of making pancake

Put it on a frying pan over low heat, lightly oil it and wipe it.

Flip it over when bubbles appear while baking.

If you repeat this, you will have delicious pancakes!
